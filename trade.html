Trade Assurance

At ELE MAX, we prioritize the security and satisfaction of our customers. Our Trade Assurance service ensures that your orders are protected from payment to delivery, giving you peace of mind when shopping on our platform.

What is Trade Assurance?

Trade Assurance is a set of protection measures provided by ELE MAX to ensure that your transactions are secure. With Trade Assurance, you can be confident that you are protected if there are any issues with your order.

How Does It Work?

Trade Assurance covers key aspects of your order:

  • Payment Protection: Your payment is protected until you confirm that you have received your order as expected.
  • Quality Protection: If the quality of your order does not match the agreed-upon standards, you are eligible for a refund.
  • Shipping Protection: Ensures that your order is shipped on time as per the agreed terms. If there is a delay, you can apply for a refund.

Secure Payment

We offer secure payment options to ensure your transactions are safe:

  • Pay securely via credit card.
  • Pay securely via bank transfer.

Benefits of Trade Assurance

Using Trade Assurance on ELE MAX provides several benefits:

  • Secure payments through our trusted payment gateway.
  • Confidence in receiving products that meet your quality expectations.
  • Peace of mind with timely shipping and delivery guarantees.
  • Access to our dedicated customer service team to assist with any issues.
